About TextUtils

TextUtils is a smart and modern text utility app built by Samridhi Prakash. It helps you analyze, manipulate, and transform your text with powerful features like:

  • Convert to UPPERCASE/lowercase
  • Bold, Italic, Underline formatting
  • Text Aestheticify ✨
  • Palindrome Checker
  • Live word/character counter and reading time
  • Dark mode with purple gradient

Whether you’re a student, content creator, or just love clean UI — TextUtils makes your text smarter!
